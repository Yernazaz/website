About Us

The environmental online school project - Ainalab was created with the support of the British Council and Chevron in the city of Atyrau, under the initiative of eco-activist Togzhan Uazikhanova. The main goal of the project is to create an accessible course for a healthy and environmentally friendly lifestyle, consisting of educational videos with practical examples and reinforcing tasks, and accessible to both schoolchildren and a wide audience interested in this topic.
